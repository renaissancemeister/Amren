Jared Taylor to speak at Texas A&M

AR editor Jared Taylor will speak at Texas A&M University on Tuesday, April 1, 2008. The subject of his talk will be “Diversity: Strength or Weakness.”

He will speak at 8:30 p.m. in Room 601, Rudder Tower, on the campus at College Station, Texas. Admission is free.

A map of the campus is available here. Rudder Tower is on Joe Routt Boulevard at Throckmorton Street.
