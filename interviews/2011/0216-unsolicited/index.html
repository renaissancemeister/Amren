Jared Taylor live on The Unsolicited Opinion

Jared Taylor, founder of the magazine, American Renaissance, will be a guest on "The Unsolicited Opinion" radio show, Wednesday, February 16 at 8pm ET. The show airs on AM 1360 in Philadelphia (WNJC1360am.com).

Taylor's group was recently denied the right to free speech and assembly because of their interest in promoting "western civilization." Whether you agree with people or you do not, the whole foundation of freedom lies in your ability to say what you believe.

"Race is an important aspect of individual and group identity. Of all the fault lines that divide society — language, religion, class, ideology — it is the most prominent and divisive. Race and racial conflict are at the heart of the most serious challenges the Western World faces in the 21st century. The problems of race cannot be solved without adequate understanding. Attempts to gloss over the significance of race or even to deny its reality only make problems worse. Progress requires the study of all aspects of race, whether historical, cultural, or biological. This approach is known as race realism."

We will be discussing Barack Obama and his "post racial presidency," as well as, multiculturalism and as evidenced by the recent commentary made by David Cameron PM of Britain and Chancellor Angela Merkel of Germany, it does not seem to be working out so well.

Tune in and decide for yourself.
