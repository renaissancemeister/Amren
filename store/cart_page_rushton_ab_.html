Race, Evolution, and Behavior

J. Philippe Rushton

2nd Special Abridged Edition (soft cover)
$6.95

This is the condensed and simplified version of Prof. Rushton’s great work. It summarizes the important findings of the unabridged version in a handy, pocket-sized format. It lacks the scholarly apparatus and detail of interest to specialists but is an excellent introduction to the thought of one of the most original and provocative social scientists of our time. This book makes an excellent gift to people who are new to racial thinking.
