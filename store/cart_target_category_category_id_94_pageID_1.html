DVD-R discs are now available for each American Renaissance Conference back to the first one in 1994.

These DVDs are made somewhat differently than the commercial "pressed" DVDs produced by major movie studios or music video houses. The information is recorded onto the DVD instead of pressed into it. Consequently, earlier DVD players may not read them. Almost all DVD players made today should read them. If you have any trouble, return them and we will either issue a refund or replace with VHS.
