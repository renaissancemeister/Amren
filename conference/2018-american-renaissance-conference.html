2018 American Renaissance Conference

Courage and Perseverance

Attendees gathered from April 27–29 near Nashville, Tennessee, to reflect on the dramatic progress but also startling setbacks in the movement to awaken white racial consciousness, and to plot a course for the years ahead. Here is a write-up of the event.

Speakers included:

Marcus Follin — “The Defense of the West Begins with You.” (video) Mr. Follin is a YouTube blogger who goes under the name of The Golden One. He is a graduate of Uppsala University in Sweden and owns a clothing company, Legio Gloria. He believes that the struggle to defend the West begins with personal integrity and development.
Adrian Davies — “Softly, Softly, Catchee Monkey: Optics, Ethics, and the Cause.” (video) Mr. Davies is a London barrister who regularly defends thought criminals.
Nick Fuentes — “Generation Z: The Answer to the Boomer Problem.” (video) Mr. Fuentes hosts a nightly YouTube series on politics called “America First.” He was very active in coordinating volunteers for the Trump campaign.
Simon Roche — “Arise!” (video) Mr. Roche is a fourth-generation South African affiliated with the civil defense organization Suidlanders, which is devoted to the protection of non-combatant Afrikaner-Boer civilians in the event of large-scale conflict in South Africa.
Serge Trifkovic — “Migratory Jihad and the Crisis of Europe.” (video) Dr. Trifkovic is the author of Sword of the Prophet and Defeating Jihad. He is a professor of international relations the University of Banja Luka, Serbian Republic, and is Foreign Affairs Editor of Chronicles Magazine. His work has been published in everything from The Times of London to Komsomolskaya Pravda.
Jared Taylor  — “Irreconcilable Differences: We Need a Divorce.” (video) Mr. Taylor is editor of American Renaissance. He is the author of Paved With Good Intentions and White Identity: Racial Consciousness in the 21st Century. His latest book is If We Do Nothing: Essays and reviews from 25 years of white advocacy.
Douglas Whitman — “Amazing Racial Differences.” (video) In 2012, Dr. Whitman retired as a professor of biology at Illinois State University. Teaching and studying evolution led him to an understanding of the biology of racial differences.
Sam Dickson  — “A Benediction for Heretics.” (video) Mr. Dickson is an attorney who has been a advocate for our people since his college days. He has addressed every American Renaissance conference.
“Reports from the Field.” (video) Patrick Casey of Identity Evropa, John Derbyshire of VDARE, Greg Johnson of Counter-Currents, and Martin O’Toole of The Occidental Quarterly give presentations on behalf of their respective organizations.
