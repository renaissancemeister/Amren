Understanding Conservatism

2008 AR Conference

Paul Gottfried

An authoritative analysis of the American conservative “movement” and how it changed over the years.

Prof. Gottfried teaches history and political science at Elizabethtown College, and is the author of many books. His most recent is Conservatism in America: Making Sense of the American Right.
