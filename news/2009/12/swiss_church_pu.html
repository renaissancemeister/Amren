Swiss Church Puts Minarets Around Baby Jesus

AD Nieuws (Rotterdam), December 29, 2009

In the church of the Swiss Bellinzona is the traditional manger with baby Jesus surrounded by minarets.

The unusual scene to people up and think about brotherhood and human rights, after 57 percent of the Swiss recently voted against minarets on mosques.

The cribs are verses from the Bible and the Koran on the theme of water.
