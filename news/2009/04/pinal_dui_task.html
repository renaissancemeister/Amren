Pinal DUI Task Force to Target Salsa Festival

Sherry Anne Rubiano, Arizona Republic (Phoenix), April 25, 2009

The Pinal County Sheriff’s Office will conduct a DUI task force Saturday for the Maricopa Salsa Festival in Maricopa.

The task force will be in effect from 4 p.m. to midnight. The main patrol focus will be the Maricopa area.

The goal is to make the streets safer through aggressive impaired driving enforcement and continued education, according to the Sheriff’s Office.

More than 8,000 people attended the Maricopa Salsa Festival last year. This year’s event runs from 5 to 9 p.m. at Pacana Park.
