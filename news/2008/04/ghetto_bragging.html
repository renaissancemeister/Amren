Ghetto Bragging Rights Community Hold Candle Light Vigil for Eric Boyd

underprivilegedJournalism, April 16, 2008

Thousands of Ghetto Bragging Rights community members rallied together in a virtual candlelight vigil for new Ghetto Black Male Felon Bragging Rights SuperThug Eric Dwayne Boyd.

In Knoxville, a smaller gathering assembled to raise commissary money and support for the first of the Knoxville5’s illegal prosecution.

“We’ve gathered here tonight on the streets Eric ran to to smoke our weed and pour out a little liquor for him,” a Knoxville Ghetto Bragging Rights thug said.

“We can be sure that we will not see justice from the circus taking place in their halls of corruption tomorrow morning,” anti black poverty champ St. Nicolas Thief said. Thief, who has been in Knoxville to monitor the trial and coach blacks on staging theatrical social action protest robberies in protest of the city and State’s institutional poverty said he is proud of Boyd’s challenge to the court and Christian and Newsom families.

“In saying “Bring It Own” [sic] Eric said to the cluster of Knoxville’s white devils : do what you. You can’t harm me or none of us.”

In court, yesterday, Boyd again wagged his tail before the judge, jury and the condemning Knoxville white community with these words:

“I be ripping and running. . . . Walking up and down the streets of Ridgebrook, just drinking, smoking and drinking and smoking weed, that’s all I do every day. I may go to Labor Ready on occasion so I can get a little more buzz money.” — Eric Dwayne Boyd

“Dogs,” Thief spit. “They are the children of their fathers! But Boyd let them and the whole world know: the penitentiary ain’t shit for niggas. We do our work in the free world and are rewarded by God eternally. All experiences enlighten the soul of the black man and woman from our infidelities from God by intermingling with The Devil. There is no soul in white people! Never expect justice from them! Never expect righteousness from them! Throughout history and even unto this day their works are proven to be the same.

“We are those who can thank God for the darkness and His light. For we are dark but absorb His light. White people can be thankful for neither.

“Once upon a time God made His light fall on the just as well as the unjust all the same. Today God’s light burns the skin of the unjust.”

While the the 2nd Knoxville 5 Carjacker support party didn’t see the same attendance as the first, local thug and rapper Beast says they raised enough money to see to it Boyd, Cobbins, Davidson, Thomas and Coleman’s commissary books will not go empty.

Thief would not confirm whether he met with the Boyd’s younger brother, Anthony, who made their family’s first public statements about Eric’s illegal prosecution, tonight. One source said hee believes the two spoke.

Thief, who is expected to depart Knoxville after tomorrow’s verdict, said he will return for the future trials “if fire and brimstone haven’t consumed the whites of this damned city by then.”
