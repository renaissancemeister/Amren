The ‘Day of the Conservative European Youth’ will be organised on Sunday 4 May.

The aim of this initiative of the Flemish Interest Youth (Vlaams Belang Jongeren) is to join forces in Europe.

Details:

4 May 2008 — 10h00

Day of the Conservative European Youth
Elzenveld-Antwerp
*  European fair
*  Workshop
*  Gigs
*  Speeches
*  Keynote speakers: Philip Claeys and Carl Lang

Sunday 4 May 2008

*  10h00: doors open
*  10h30: start workshops
*  11h00: start European fair
*  11h00: gigs / speeches
*  13h00: conclusion workshops
*  15h30: presentation of the resolutions to the press
*  15h45: presentation of the resolutions to the public
*  16h00: Keynote speakers
