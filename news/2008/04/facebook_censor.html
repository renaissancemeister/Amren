Facebook Censorship

American Renaissance Special Report, April 9, 2008

A reader reports that if you try to post a link to American Renaissance on the wall of a Facebook profile, Facebook rejects it.

The message that comes up is as follows:

“Content Rejected: Some of the content you included in your message is not allowed by Facebook.”

Readers are encouraged to let us know of similar experiences on websites. We also want to know when readers find that a website had changed its policy to permit links to AR.

Send your account or update to:

banned@amren.com
