Bordering on Insanity

Sam Rosenbaum, New York Post, August 19, 2008

Linda Chavez’s reaction to the latest Census Bureau predictions of the United States becoming a majority non-white nation by 2042 is pure delight (“The Return of the Melting Pot,” PostOpinion, Aug. 16).

However, this unprecedented change in demographics, due to massive legal and illegal Third World immigration, is nothing more than a form of ethnic cleansing.

More specifically, the same thing that happened to American Indians is now occurring to America’s white population.

Foreigners are colonizing large parts of America; Spanish is becoming a de facto official language; the illegal drug trade is largely an alien endeavor, and, like the Indians, whites no longer have any control over their national destiny.

It is a criminal act for anyone to criticize these changes.
