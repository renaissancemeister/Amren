President of Rapides Parish NAACP Arrested

AP, KATC-3 (LA)

Sheriff’s deputies have arrested the president of the Rapides Parish branch of the NAACP on drug and weapons charges.

Authorities say 57-year-old Joe Buckner Senior was booked early yesterday on several charges including three counts of firearm possession by a felon and one count of conspiring to produce-or-manufacture cocaine.

Bond was set at 90-thousand-250 dollars. Sergeant Phillip Jordan of the Rapides Parish sheriff’s office said he couldn’t release details about the case because it remains under investigation. Buckner says he will release a statement tomorrow.

Last October, Buckner was arrested on suspicion of harassing an Alexandria couple. He declared at the time that the accusations are — quote — a big lie and that his attorney was handling it.
