Fox News, June 18, 2010

The Obama administration plans to file suit challenging Arizona’s immigration law, officials told Fox News on Friday.

{snip} But an official told Fox News on Friday that while the review is still underway, the decision has already been made that a Department of Justice suit will be filed. The administration at this point is just building its case.
