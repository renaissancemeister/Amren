Offensive Sears Ad Gone After Angry Whites Complain

Western Voices World News, November 24, 2010

A small victory like this can logically lead to bigger and better ones.

A racially provocative Christmas commercial aired by Sears has disappeared after angry whites left negative remarks about the ad on YouTube.

The commercial, which featured a black man repeatedly kissing a blonde white girl under mistletoe, was broadcast on all three networks during morning news shows. The ad was posted on YouTube where it received over 15,000 views. Comments left by vistors to the YouTube site were overwhelmingly negative, and ranged between harsh to hostile. After the commercial stopped running, the YouTube version also disappeared.
