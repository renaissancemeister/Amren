Illegal Immigrant Sentenced for Stash House Robbery

Susan Carroll, Houston Chronicle, November 5, 2010

A federal judge today sentenced an illegal immigrant from Honduras to more than eight years in prison for trying to rip off a smuggling load at gunpoint.

According to federal invesigators, Kenneth Barahona, 19, was wearing an Immigration and Customs Enforcement T-shirt when he tried to abduct up to 30 illegal immigrants from a Houston stash house at gunpoint.
