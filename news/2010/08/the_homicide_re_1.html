The Homicide Report

Los Angeles Times, August 1, 2010

Go to the original site to make use of the interactivity features. When you click on one of the brown circles, you get a map of the crime scene and a list of the people killed in that area showing their age and the date killed.

There are charts breaking homicides down (January 1, 2007 to August 1, 2010) by weapon, by cause, by race (or ethnicity), by age, and by day of the week (Saturdays and Sundays are big days, followed by Monday).

There are also photos of the victims.
