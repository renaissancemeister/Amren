Council of Conservative Citizens, 2010 National Conference

Council of Conservative Citizens

The Council of Conservative Citizens (CofCC), America’s premier conservative activist group, will hold its annual conference Friday, June 4, and Saturday, June 5, in middle Tennessee. An outstanding lineup of speakers is sure to make this the best CofCC conference ever, and we encourage all those who can attend next weekend to do so. Many of the conference’s 18 speakers will be familiar to AR readers, including:

Jared Taylor, editor of American Renaissance, who will address the Saturday luncheon.

Sam G. Dickson, Esq., 1994-2010 AR conference speaker, on “Myths Liberals Live By.”

Louis T. March, Esq., 2010 AR Conference speaker, on “What Shall Conservatives Work to Conserve.”

Paul Fromm, 2004 and 2010 AR Conference speaker, and Canada’s leading defender of free speech.

Miles Wolpin, PhD, Emeritus Professor of Political Science, SUNY Potsdam, on “Overcoming Demonization and Political Correctness.”

Tomislav Sunic, PhD, on “Cultural or Political Hegemony? The Case of the USA and Europe.”

James Edwards, host of the Political Cesspool radio program.

And many more!

Conference registration is $45 per person and includes the Saturday luncheon. Additional family members can attend for $40 each. For more information or to register, please call the CofCC at 636-940-8474. Click here to join the CofCC.
