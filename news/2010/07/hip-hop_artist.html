Hip-Hop Artist Nelly to Perform in Branson

KMOV-TV (St. Louis), July 6, 2010

Branson [Missouri] will expand its musical offerings when hip-hop artist and rapper Nelly performs at an open-air concert on Aug. 21.

{snip}

Paul Dunn, manager of the Grand Palace, says Nelly’s show will be the first hip-hop concert performed in Branson. Dunn tells KSPR that he plans to bring a wider variety of new, famous artists to the restort town, which is generally known for its country, bluegrass and gospel shows.
