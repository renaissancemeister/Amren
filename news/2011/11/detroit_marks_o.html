Detroit Marks Opening of Expanded African School

M Live, November 16, 2011

The Detroit school system is holding a celebration to mark the opening of a 161,000-square-foot addition to the Marcus Garvey Academy, an east side school with an African-centered curriculum for students from kindergarten through eighth grade.

The district says the opening ceremony for the $2.1 million Harambee Center will be Wednesday from 5-7 p.m.
