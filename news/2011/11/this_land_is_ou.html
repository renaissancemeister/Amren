This Land Is Our Land — For More Than Forty Thousand Years

Mark Pritchard, BNP Ideas, November 3, 2011

New scientific findings have shown that the ancestors of today’s British people have lived here more than twice as long as was previously thought.

Results of new analysis of surrounding fossils and rocks, published in this week’s edition of the scientific journal of record, Nature, prove that the fossil teeth of one of our ancestors, found in Kent’s Cavern, near Torquay in Devon in 1927, are at least 41,000 years old. Double their previous estimated age.

The fossils are of a modern White human, who suitably barbered and attired could walk unremarked through a shopping centre anywhere in Britain. Genetic analysis has already proved all genuinely indigenous Britons can trace their ancestry back to this Palaeolithic population, the first modern humans ever to live here.

We may have additional blood from later waves of North-West European invaders — Celts, Saxons, Vikings, Normans etc., each wave vigorously resisted by the natives — but if you are genuinely a native Briton, some of your ancestors lived here when the Great Ice made a wall across England a mile high. If you are an Immigrant, born here or otherwise, they didn’t.

In one celebrated study, for example, DNA studies showed a schoolteacher in Cheddar, Somerset, was a direct descendant of fossil humans found nearby at Cheddar Gorge. Many other genetic analyses and results also prove our descent from our Ice Age British ancestors.

Until now it was thought that these First Britons arrived after the last, or Devensian, glacial advance had retreated from Britain, about 12-14,000 years ago. However, in 2009, further radiocarbon isotope analysis at Oxford University revealed that one of the oldest Britons, the so-called “Red Lady of Paviland” (actually a young man ceremonially buried), was 33,000 years old, more than twice as old as had been thought.

Now it appears our ancestors were here thousands of years even before that. They would have moved southward as the glaciers advanced after 22,000 BC, but after the last glaciation’s midwinter 18,000 years ago they would have moved back north again, reclaiming our homeland from the Ice mile by mile, year by year, generation by generation, in a thousands of years long war against the Winter of the World. A war in which our ancestors won our right to our land for ever.

Yet again science proves we are NOT a “Nation of Immigrants”. Our ancestors were the first modern people ever to live here, and we have lived here now for over four hundred centuries. For at least forty-one thousand years, almost ten times the span of all recorded history, this land has been our land. We must not lose it, still less must we give it up, now.
