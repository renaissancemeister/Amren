2011 Council of Conservative Citizens National Conference

Council of Conservative Citizens, May 2011

The 2011 C of CC National Conference will take place Friday and Saturday, June 10 and 11, at a private venue in Winston-Salem, North Carolina. Speakers will start at 1:00 PM Friday. There will be a live band Friday night. Speeches will run from 9:00 AM — 5:00 PM Saturday. This includes a special luncheon featuring Jared Taylor and Louis T. March.

Registration is $55 per person or $100 per couple. This includes the Saturday luncheon. We have a very nice group rate of $68 a night at the hotel.

Rooms are limited so conference goers should reserve their rooms immediately. All members should have received the Council Reporter newsletter with detail on reserving your room. If you are not a member and would like to attend, please call 636-940-8474.
