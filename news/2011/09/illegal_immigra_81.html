Illegal Immigrant Arrested for 6th OUI

My Fox Boston, September 26, 2011

Police arrested an illegal immigrant for Operating Under the Influence. It’s his 6th offense, something he’d been deported for previously.

{snip}

AIFS identified him as Eduardo Torres. The 48-year-old was living in Marlborough.

It was determined that Torres was a previously deported fugitive wanted by Homeland Security and ICE. Torres has five previous OUI convictions, three in California and two in Massachusetts. He later failed a Breathalyzer test.

{snip}

This arrest comes on the heels of controversy over another illegal immigrant who is accused of drunk driving and hitting and killing a young Milford man.
