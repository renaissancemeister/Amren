Exit Poll: Whites Are Different

National Journal, January 6, 2011

Previously unreleased results from the 2010 exit polls show a stark gap between whites and minorities and a smaller but still significant difference between blue- and white-collar whites.

National Journal has published an interesting racial analysis of the results of the 2010 elections. It is not as good as the AR cover story for the January 2011 issue, but it is a good start.
