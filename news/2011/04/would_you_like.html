Would You Like to Write for Teaching Tolerance?

Lisa Ann Williamson, Teaching Tolerance (SPLC), April 1, 2011

Teaching Tolerance wants to expand its corps of bloggers. We are looking for teachers, administrators and school counselors who can reflect on day-to-day school experiences. Candidates should be familiar with Teaching Tolerance’s mission: to promote respect for differences and an appreciation of diversity in the classroom and beyond.

Priority will be given to educators who have written a blog previously. However, other editorial writing experience is a plus.

{snip}

If you’re interested, contact us at editor@TeachingTolerance.org. {snip}

Teaching Tolerance pays $50 per published post. Some writers will receive more money if they’re able to produce at least three posts per month. {snip}
