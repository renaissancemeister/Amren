Nevada Home to Largest Share of Illegal Workers

Cristina Silva, Bloomberg, February 2, 2011

Nevada has the largest share of illegal workers in the nation, with roughly 10 percent of its work force not authorized by the federal government, according to the Pew Hispanic Center, a nonpartisan research group.

The report published Tuesday shows a slight jump in illegal workers as Nevada’s job market has shrunk amid record unemployment. Nevada’s population of illegal workers was 9.4 percent in 2009. The Silver State’s unemployment rate has since climbed to 14.5 percent, the highest in the nation.

Nevada also has the largest share of illegal immigrants at 7.2 percent. Only 3.7 percent of people in the United States are illegal immigrants.

Nevada’s tourism-rich economy lured foreign workers for years with promises of plentiful jobs in its construction and service industries. Immigrants urged friends and relatives to relocate to Nevada with their stories of fair wages. Employers fueled the migration, sometimes sending headhunters to Mexico and other Latin American nations to recruit affordable laborers.

I lived in Nevada for damned near thirty years, and I can tell you exactly how these illegals came to be in the state. When I first moved to Nevada, Howard Hughes was still the basic reigning economic force in the state. The mafia was in its last stages of going legit, realizing that there was more to be made by simply expanding and investing capital than by skimming profits. Most casino jobs, other than dealing or being a pit boss, were fairly low paying. Las Vegas was still, for most practical purposes, still segregated, with blacks living in “northtown” and working in casinos as janitors and busboys, or other menial jobs.

Then, entrepreneurs like Steve Wynn and others began to transform Vegas into a “family” destination, with sprawling, family friendly casinos with entertainment themes. The streets around the casinos were always safe, and business boomed. Mormons, many of them left over from the Howard Hughes days, controlled their fair share of management positions and helped in this boom, steering competent mormon workers into mid level wage earning positions. I knew of quite a few non mormon kids who deliberately “knocked up” mormon girls in high school, in order to facilitate a “shotgun” wedding and a subsequent entry level casino career type job. I am not disparaging Mormons — they take care of their own, and I have always known them to be clean, hardworking people who are very tolerant of others and their faiths.

What this did, however, was create a job environment with jobs that — I won’t call it jobs that white people wouldn’t do, but more like whites could make much more money doing something else. When I was in high school, minimum wage was $3.35 an hour, and that is what busboys made. I knew plenty of white kids who bussed tables, and there were no illegals doing this work. What happened, though, was that the economy simply boomed. Busboys could make $7 an hour in the late nineties, but dishwashers could make $9. I knew kids that had dropped out of high school that were making as much as teachers. I had a friend who barely made it through high school who was making $30 an hour managing one of the “motion” rides at one of the casinos. There was more work to do, literally, than there were workers. So…

The illegals started pouring in. They took over all of the “low” paying jobs. The relatively few blacks in Las Vegas seemed content with welfare. The white trash who didn’t want to work existed on the sidelines selling meth. Everything was great — everyone who wanted to work had a job, the housing market was booming.

No one seemed to notice, however, that the quality of life was deteriorating rapidly. I moved from Las Vegas to Reno to go to college in 1984. I was literally shocked the first time I saw a bum passed out in front of a slot machine. That would never have happened in the “old” Las Vegas of Howard Hughes and the mob. They would have taken him outside, beat him, and stolen his shoes to teach him a lesson. While the mob had an extremely negative influence on Las Vegas, they did keep the streets safe for tourists. Petty robbery and such were bad for business, and practically did not exist.

With the dissolution of mob influence in Las Vegas, however, things went rapidly downhill. Black gangs from LA began exporting their influence to Las Vegas. Parts of the city rapidly turned into “no fly” zones. The progeny of the illegals found it more lucrative to turn to gangs and selling drugs than to legitimate work.

Reno, where I lived for a number of years, never quite degenerated to the level of Las Vegas, but it was a microcosm of the effects of illegals as well. Mexicans like cold weather about as much as blacks do, but they still managed to make it up north in significant numbers.

When the financial meltdown of 2009 hit Nevada, it basically melted down a monstrous ponzi scheme. Tourists quit coming, and an economy based on tourism collapsed. Artificially inflated union wages started finally choking the casinos, as they did the auto industry. I talked to a friend in Reno recently, and he told me that official numbers are too low, that unemployment there is over twenty percent, and it’s worse in Las Vegas. The casinos are tanking.

Trouble is brewing in Nevada. Unlike other states where the illegals are fleeing for greener pastures, the ones in Nevada seem to be staying put. That is ominous.
