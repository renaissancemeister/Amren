Protest in Baldwin Park

Charles Kirkby, What’s Right With America (Channel 17, Santa Barbara), May 18, 2005

I started the day expecting to cover the “Baldwin Park monument.” The monument, although still important that day, became a secondary issue. The anti-American, anti-Caucasian counter protest is what truly became the news of the day.

Screams of “death to the rednecks” and “Zarqawi the Gringo killer, yes!, yes!” displayed the mood and the message of the counter protesters. I particularly remember when our group of “Gringos” was told to get out of the shade and into the sun so we could get skin cancer and die.

This was not about Illegal Immigrants who want to do the jobs that no American will do. This was about Reconquista, revolution and taking back the Western United States for Mexico.

Fortunately, I have my own forum to broadcast this disturbing footage and report the truth about what happened. “What’s Right With America!” is a live, weekly television program in Santa Barbara, Ca. It airs every Wednesday night from 8-9 PM on Channel 17. I look forward to further exposing this explosive and important story.

I will be making my best attempt to cover this growing controversy wherever it may happen. If the major media won’t show it, you can bet that I will. I believe the American people have the right to know the truth about the pro-Illegal Immigration movement.
