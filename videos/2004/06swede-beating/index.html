Attack on Swedes by Black Immigrants

In June 2004, a gang of African immigrants beat up two Swedish men who asked them to stop vandalizing the city’s flower pots. The blacks filmed the attack and laughingly commented on it. Eventually, the tape came into the hands of the Swedish police. The police broadcast the video on television in November, and viewers identified one of the attackers as a professional soccer player.
