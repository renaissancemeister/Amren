Bus attack

Source: fdesouche.com/articles/33981

On the night of December 6, 2008, an RATP bus rider was the victim of a pickpocketing committed by several individuals, one of them searching his pockets for a second attempt to divert his attention. Trying to recover his property, he was violently thrown to the ground and attacked.
